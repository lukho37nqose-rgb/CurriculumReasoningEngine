Workflow

A curriculum change is a governed release, not an overwrite

  1. 1Draft

    Record the proposed change and source.

  2. 2Submit

    Departmental editor identifies affected records.

  3. 3Validate

    References, totals and regression tests are checked.

  4. 4Approve

    A different authorised role accepts the release.

  5. 5Publish

    A new version is issued; history is not rewritten.

  6. 6Supersede

    Older cohorts remain attached to their governing rules.
