Reasoning model

Four kinds of claims are kept apart.

01Transcript facts

What the academic record appears to say: attempts, marks, grades, programme text and years.

02Curriculum rules

What the selected handbook route requires and which courses or majors belong within its scope.

03Computed conclusions

What follows when represented rules are applied to represented evidence.

04Institutional judgment

What cannot be inferred: concessions, substitutions, capacity, placement evidence and discretionary decisions.
